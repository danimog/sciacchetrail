[:it](Monterosso al Mare - 11 Feb. 2018). Siamo super entusiasti di avere con noi a Sciacchetrail 2018 Amanda Basham  atleta di Altra Running e GORE RUNNING WEAR athlete. Dopo la vittoria, diversi anni fa, al suo esordio nelle Ultra distanze (un 50k), ha continuato a mietere successi in gare di tutte le distanze, inclusa una vittoria a USATF 50 Mile Trail Championships in 2015, un quarto posto alla prestigiosa Western States 100 nel 2016,  e un post nella top 20 alla super competitiva UTMB 2017.  Nel 2017  ha vinto diverse gare compresa la Ultra Race of Champions 100k.  Ha iniziato la stagione 2018 con un secondo posto a Tarawera Ultramarathon 100K in Nuova Zelanda.  Oltre ad essere una runner professionista, Amanda e' anche coach e lavora a tempo pieno per iFit . Vive e si allena a Logan, Utah, USA.
Photo: @joshuatug[:en](Monterosso al Mare - 11 Feb. 2018). We’re so excited to have Altra Running and GORE RUNNING WEAR athlete Amanda Basham (Amanda Basham) at the starting line of Sciacchetrail 2018. After winning her first ever ultra trail race (a 50k) a few years ago, she has continued to excel at races of all distances, including a win at the USATF 50 Mile Trail Championships in 2015, 4th at the prestigious Western States 100 in 2016, and a top 20 finish at the highly competitive 2017 UTMB. In 2017 she won several US races including the Ultra Race of Champions 100k.  She started out the 2018 season with a second place finish at the Tarawera Ultramarathon 100K in New Zealand.  In addition to running professionally, Amanda also runs her own coaching business and works full time for iFit. She lives and trains in Logan, Utah, USA.
