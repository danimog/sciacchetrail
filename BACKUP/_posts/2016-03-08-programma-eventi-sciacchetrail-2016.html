From Friday 18 until Sunday 20 March, wine and typicalities, meetings, training and much more in Monterosso, arrival and departure point of the 2nd edition of the ultra trail on the wine trails of the Cinque Terre

Friday 18 March 2016

Monterosso City Hall - Council Hall, 4pm
Product and Landscape: heroic viticulture in coastal and mountain areas - Round table organized by CERVIM and Cinque Terre National Park. (Event valid for continuing education for journalists).

Saturday 19 March 2016

Monterosso, Piazza Garibaldi 10am - 6pm
Wine and Food Stands of the Cinque Terre, Tuscan-Emilian Apennines National Park, Lunigiana, Val di Vara and Outdoor Expo organized by Cinque Terre Trekking

Monterosso City Hall, Piazza Garibaldi 11am
Running and Wine: emotions to recount - Meeting with Wine Trails
SciaccheTrail, Trail del Moscato and Valtellina Wine Trail and their respective producers organized by Skialper magazine and Cinque Terre National Park
(Event valid for continuing education for journalists).
Saturday 19 March 2016
Monterosso, (Fegina promenade) 10am - 6pm
Artisan market
Monterosso, (City Hall grounds) 4:30pm
Yoga session with Maestro Malcolm Bilotta
Weather permitting the session will be held on the beach. (Only for SciaccheTrail runners with a reservation).
Monterosso, (Music Hall, near City Hall) 4:30pm-7pm
Race bib and gift bag pick-up organized by Mysdam

Monterosso, City Hall (ground floor hall) 6pm
SciaccheTrail pre-race technical briefing

Monterosso, Event Tent - Piazzetta Belvedere, 12 noon - 6pm
Wine & Food with the Wine Trails: producers of Vino Doc Cinque Terre, Consorzio Sciacchetrà, Cantina Sociale Cinque Terre, Associazione La Bottega del Vino Moscato d'Asti, Consorzio di Tutela Vini Valtellina. Gastronomy and local street food.
4:30pm guided wine tasting with the Wine Trails organized by Associazione Italiana Sommelier - La Spezia delegation, reservation required

Monterosso, Piazzetta Belvedere (near La Sportiva Arc), 2:15pm
Race bib pick-up for MiniSciaccheTrail

3pm Start MiniSciaccheTrail
“Madrina” of the event: Emmie Collinge, mountain running vice world champion. Partecipation by reservation only.
