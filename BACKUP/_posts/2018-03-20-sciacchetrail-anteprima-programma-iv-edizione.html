Ci siamo quasi, ASD Cinque Terre, Cinque Terre Trekking e La Sportiva hanno le idee chiare. Sciacchetrail sarà un lungo week end di emozioni.

Immancabile, dopo il successo dello scorso anno, lo Sciacchetrail Outdoor & Family Village con nuovi sport all’aria aperta da provare per tutto il weekend ed eventi collegati al mondo del vino, del cibo e dell’arte come il concorso pittorico ArTrail, dedicato alla valorizzazione del territorio e delle sue identità.

[caption id="attachment_5860" align="alignleft" width="300"] Brother's Surf Club Levanto[/caption]

Tra le attività previste: climb station, skateboard, stretching a cura di Officina Posture e Blackroll Italia, poledance, rowing, slacke line, mountain bike, table boulder, yoga e gioca yoga, laboratori artistici e tanto altro ancora.

We're almost there and ASD Cinque Terre,  Cinque Terre Trekking e La Sportiva ideas are clear. Sciacchetrail will be a long weekend of excitement.

Not to be missed, after the success of last year, the Sciacchetrail Outdoor & Family Village, with new sports and activities to try all weekend and events connected to the worlds of wine, food and art, like the painting competition  ArTrail, dedicated to the enhancement of the territory and its identity.

Among the planned activities: climb station, skateboard, stretching by Officina Posture and Blackroll Italia, poledance, rowing, slack line, mountain bike, table boulder, art laboratories and much more.

6:00pm meet-up with elites and trail running ambassadors at Cinque Terre Trekking in Manarola

Friday March 23rd 

12 noon | Vernazza City Hall, Sciacchetrail inauguration, with elite runners, race organizers, and institutions with Cinque Terre DOC wine tasting.

4:30pm Briefing pre gara | Monterosso City Hall

5:00pm  Race bib and gift bag pick-up untill 7:30pm | Monterosso City Hall

6:00pm Top runners and Top winemakers: a dialogue on wine and running. Running elites and local winemakers discuss their passion.  A tasting of Cinque Terre DOC wine organized by Sommelier AIS La Spezia will stimulate the discussion | Event tent in Piazza Belvedere (booking and info: info@enotecatecainternazionale.com max 40 partecipants).
